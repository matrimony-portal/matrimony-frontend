Getting Started

How do I create an account? +
Click on "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your basic details including name, email, phone number, and create a password. Complete your profile by adding personal, educational, and family details. Upload clear photos to make your profile attractive. Your profile will be verified within 24 hours.
Is registration free? +
Yes! Registration on Perfect Match is completely free. You can create your profile, browse limited profiles, and send limited interests without any charge. Premium memberships are available with additional features.
How long does profile verification take? +
Profile verification typically takes 24-48 hours. Our team manually reviews each profile to ensure authenticity. You'll receive an email notification once your profile is verified. Verified profiles get better visibility and more responses.

Profile & Privacy

How can I edit my profile? +
Login to your account, go to "My Profile" or "Edit Profile" from the dashboard. You can update your personal details, educational qualifications, career information, family details, and preferences. Don't forget to save your changes.
Can I hide my profile temporarily? +
Yes! Go to Settings > Privacy > Profile Visibility and select "Hidden". Your profile won't be visible to others, but your account remains active. You can unhide it anytime by changing the visibility setting back to "Everyone" or "Premium Members Only".
Who can see my contact details? +
By default, your contact details are visible only to premium members and those whose interest you have accepted. You can change this in Settings > Privacy > Show Contact Details. For better security, we recommend keeping it restricted.
How do I delete my account? +
Go to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including profile, photos, and messages will be permanently deleted. You can also choose to deactivate temporarily instead.

Matching & Search

How does the matching algorithm work? +
Our smart matching algorithm considers multiple factors including age, education, location, religion, profession, family background, and your stated preferences. It uses machine learning to suggest the most compatible profiles based on your activity and preferences.
How do I send interest to someone? +
Browse profiles using search or view recommended matches. Click on "Send Interest" or "Send Proposal" button on the profile you like. You can add a personalized message (premium feature). The person will be notified and can accept or decline your interest.
What should I do if I receive inappropriate messages? +
You can block the user immediately from their profile or chat. Report the profile with details and screenshots if possible. Our team takes such reports seriously and will take appropriate action within 24 hours. You can also contact our support team.
